Keyboard first, touch playable

Press Space or tap to launch

Arrows or A / D move. Space, click, or tap fires. The on-screen buttons work on touch. Press M to mute and R to restart.

How to play
Controls
← →

Slide the ship left and right.

Space

Fire upward. Keep the lane open.

M

Toggle sound. The tones are simple and deliberately short.

R

Restart immediately after a rough run.

What this phase includes

A formation that enters from above, breaks into dive attacks, shoots back, awards points, tracks lives, and ramps difficulty wave by wave.

No tractor beam, no boss fights, no long menu flow. Just the loop.

Update ritual

Tweak js/galaga.js, play a few waves, run a syntax check, then push to master so GitHub Pages can redeploy.

Best score is stored locally so the run keeps a little memory between refreshes.

For repo-side controls and the edit checklist, see PLAY.md.
